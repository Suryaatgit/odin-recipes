Some fun facts about lasagna

Lasagne is a wide, flat pasta shape and possibly one of the oldest. As with most other types of pasta, the word is a plural form. Lasagne meaning more than one piece of lasagna ribbon. The word also refers to a dish made with this type of pasta, in several layers interspersed with layers of ingredients and sauces
